## OpenAI Releases GPT Image 1.5

**TLDR:** OpenAI has launched GPT Image 1.5, a completely redesigned image generation experience within ChatGPT, aimed at competing with Google's Nano Banana Pro. The new model offers precise editing, a new Creative Studio, and 4x faster generation.

**Summary:**
The AI model wars are escalating, with OpenAI and Google trading blows. OpenAI has just released GPT Image 1.5, a significant upgrade to its image generation capabilities. This move is seen as a direct response to the viral success of Google's Nano Banana Pro. The new model allows for precise editing of images, preserving lighting, composition, and facial likeness while only changing what the user requests. It also introduces a new "Creative Studio" in ChatGPT, providing a more intuitive and visual way to create and edit images.

Meanwhile, Google is not standing still. The company has released Gemini 3 Flash, a new model that offers "frontier intelligence at Flash-level speed" and is now the default model in the Gemini app. Google is also integrating its vibe-coding tool, Opal, into the Gemini web app. Other notable updates include Kling AI 2.6's improved motion control for video generation and Meta AI glasses' new "Conversation Focus" feature. For developers and creators, this rapid pace of innovation means more powerful and accessible tools are constantly becoming available.

**Key takeaways:**
- OpenAI's GPT Image 1.5 offers precise image editing and a new Creative Studio.
- Google has released Gemini 3 Flash and integrated Opal into the Gemini web app.
- Kling AI 2.6 has improved motion control for AI video generation.
- Meta AI glasses have a new "Conversation Focus" feature and Spotify integration.
